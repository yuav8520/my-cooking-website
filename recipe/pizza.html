For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F or 43°C)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the Pizza Sauce:

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, sliced mushrooms, sliced onions, black olives, etc.)

Instructions:

For the Pizza Dough:

In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  2. Stir the ingredients together until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment for this step.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

For the Pizza Sauce:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.
  3. Remove the sauce from heat and set it aside.

Putting It All Together:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Punch down the risen pizza dough and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet that's been dusted with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.
  4. Spread a generous amount of the pizza sauce over each dough round, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Carefully transfer the pizzas onto the preheated pizza stone or onto a baking sheet if you're not using a stone.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  • Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a minute before slicing. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
  • Feel free to customize this pizza with your favorite ingredients and get creative with different toppings and cheese varieties. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
