Ingredients:

Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups (about 200 grams) of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup (60 grams) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115 grams) unsalted butter, melted

filling:

  • 16 ounces (450 grams) cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (60 grams) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

topping: (not necessary)

  • Fresh berries, fruit compote, chocolate chips, or your choice of toppings

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Crust:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are evenly coated.
    • Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch (23 cm) springform pan or a pie dish to create an even crust layer. You can use the back of a spoon or the bottom of a glass to help compact it.
    • Place the crust in the refrigerator while you prepare the filling.

  2. making the filling:

    • In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes.
    • In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
    • Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract to the cream cheese and mix until well combined.
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until it's fully incorporated, creating a smooth and airy filling.

  3. now we assemble :)

    • Remove the crust from the refrigerator and pour the cheesecake filling over the crust.
    • Smooth the top with a spatula to create an even surface.

  4. now we wait:

    • Cover the cheesecake with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least 4-6 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow it to set.

  5. final touches

    • Before serving, you can add your choice of toppings. Fresh berries, fruit compote, chocolate chips, or whipped cream are popular options.
    • Carefully remove the cheesecake from the springform pan (if using) and slice into portions.
    • Serve chilled and enjoy your delicious no-bake cheesecake!

This no-bake cheesecake is a crowd-pleaser and is perfect for any occasion. It's creamy, rich, and doesn't require an oven, making it a great dessert option when you want to keep things simple.
